The world has changed a lot in the past year, but has it changed enough?

While the open access movement has made great strides in science, the path to open research in the humanities and social sciences remains less clear.

Here at CC headquarters, we’re always eager to accomplish more our limited staff and budget allow. We want to build tools to help CC licensors track and measure reuse of their content, but we haven’t yet figured out how to fund and sustain those tools.

And in some parts of the world, open is a dangerous proposition. In Syria, CC community leader Bassel Khartabil has been in prison for over two years, but there have still been no formal charges brought against him.
