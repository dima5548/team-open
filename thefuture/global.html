Licenses don’t change the world. People change the world.

The Creative Commons community is growing every day. We’re in Finland building a new CC plugin for Wordpress, in Japan hosting an open data symposium, in Kenya hosting School of Open workshops, and in Morocco running booksprints.

The change we believe in in requires more than individual license users. It requires buy-in from governments, institutitons, NGOs, and IGOs, and the CC Affiliate Network is shepherding that change on a worldwide scale.
